ABOUT RISING DISPATCH

Rising Dispatch was founded as a partnership in 1997 to initially operate same day deliveries in small vans throughout the USA from its Ipswich base. Rising Dispatch grew its customer base and delivery services and became a Limited company in August 1997.
Increasing its services and deliveries considerably since then, it has seen the need to relocate twice within Ipswich.
Rising Dispatch has successfully teamed up with APC Overnight and has the ability to offer further network services, which now also include overnight deliveries for larger/heavier items via our pallet network, international deliveries, via airfreight, and dedicated same day delivery for anything that can be accommodated on a motorcycle up to 3.5 tonnes. Having been an established family run business for over 30 years, we provide a friendly and reliable service. We can help every customer in a very personal way as there is always a member of the dedicated customer services team available to talk during operational hours. We are also members of the Road Haulage Association.
